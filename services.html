Frequently Asked Questions

Material choice depends on your local climate. Composite decking works well in most climates and requires minimal maintenance. Pressure-treated lumber is cost-effective but needs regular maintenance. Tropical hardwoods like Ipe are premium options for harsh climates but require higher investment.

Most deck construction projects require permits, especially for structures over 30 inches high or attached to your home. Our contractors handle permit applications and ensure all work meets local building codes.

With proper construction and maintenance, pressure-treated decks last 10-15 years, composite decks 25-30 years, and premium hardwood decks can last 50+ years. Regular maintenance extends the life of any deck significantly.
